What If Daddy Were A Dinosaur!?

When grown-up life gets busy and boring, just imagine what fun it would be if Daddy were a dinosaur!

Makes a Great Gift for Dads!

buy it on amazon

If your child loves dinosaurs and you’re looking for a heartwarming children’s picture book about kids and their dads, What If Daddy Were a Dinosaur? is the perfect bedtime read or Father’s Day gift.

Bret Manley is a devoted dad, loving husband, and former Officer in the U.S. Naval Reserve who understands the magic of bedtime stories that spark joy and wonder. His love for dinosaurs and fatherhood inspired What If Daddy Were a Dinosaur?—a book that captures the thrill of childhood imagination and the special connection between parent and child. When he’s not dreaming up prehistoric adventures, Bret enjoys playing anything with his kids (but especially dinosaurs), singing and playing in a band, amateur photography, and gardening. He and his wife and two children live in Washington, D.C.

Ira Baykovska ​is an acclaimed children’s book illustrator and a mom of two beautiful girls. She has been drawing for as long as she can remember and sometimes cannot believe that this hobby has becom​e her life-long career. She has been working as a freelance illustrator since 2014 and has illustrated more than 20 books for kids. Ira has a degree in Graphic Design and currently lives and works in Lviv, Ukraine. Learn more about her work at www.baykovska.com
